# Cohortix Logo Concepts

**Product:** Cohortix (AI-native project management) **Date:** 2026-02-19
**Theme:** "Linear meets AI ops" — Dark mode, Indigo Purple (#5E6AD2), Minimal,
Tech.

## Concept 01: Geometric Icon + Wordmark

**File:** `concept-01-geometric.png` **Rationale:** This concept focuses on the
structural aspect of project management. The abstract geometric shape represents
the "cohorts" or distinct groups of agents and humans working together in a
structured grid. It uses the primary indigo accent against the dark void to pop,
signaling precision and order.

## Concept 02: Lettermark (C / Cx)

**File:** `concept-02-lettermark.png` **Rationale:** A highly stylized "C" or
"Cx" monogram. This is designed for high legibility at small sizes (favicons,
app icons). The intertwining lines within the letterform suggest the merging of
two workflows: human intuition and AI speed. It’s sleek, fast, and recognizable.

## Concept 03: Symbol (Human + AI Teamwork)

**File:** `concept-03-symbol.png` **Rationale:** This direction explores the
"neural" aspect of the product. Using nodes, orbits, or connected paths, it
visualizes the network of active agents. It's less rigid than the geometric
concept, feeling more organic and "alive," representing the AI side of the
platform.

## Concept 04: Minimal Wordmark

**File:** `concept-04-wordmark.png` **Rationale:** For a tool that likely sits
open all day, sometimes less is more. This concept relies almost entirely on the
typeface (Inter-like sans serif) in stark white (#FAFAFA). A very subtle indigo
accent (a dot, a slash, or a specially colored tittle) provides the only brand
cue. It says "utility first."

## Concept 05: Abstract Mark (Mathematical)

**File:** `concept-05-abstract.png` **Rationale:** This concept leans into the
"ops" and data side. It uses mathematical precision—perhaps a hexagon, a cube,
or a dataset visualization—to imply that Cohortix brings order to chaos. It
feels engineered and robust.

## Concept 06: Playful Option

**File:** `concept-06-playful.png` **Rationale:** While the brand is
professional, "playful" here means "approachable." This concept uses slightly
softer shapes or a more distinct, character-like abstract element. It aims to
make the AI feel friendly and helpful rather than cold and robotic,
distinguishing it from purely utilitarian dev tools.
