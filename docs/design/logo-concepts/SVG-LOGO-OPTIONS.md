# Cohortix Logo Concepts

This document outlines four hand-crafted SVG logo directions for **Cohortix**,
an AI-native project management platform. All logos utilize the primary brand
color `#5E6AD2` (Indigo-Purple) and `#FAFAFA` (Foreground White) on the assumed
dark background `#0A0A0B`.

## 01. Cohort Symbol

**Concept:** Interconnected nodes representing the "cohort" (team).

- **Design:** A triangular arrangement of three circles (nodes) connected to a
  central white node.
- **Rationale:** Symbolizes the synergy between human team members and AI agents
  working in a unified flow. The central white node acts as the "kernel" or
  "bridge" of coordination.
- **Files:** `svg-01-cohort-icon.svg`, `svg-01-cohort-wordmark.svg`

## 02. Abstract "C" (Lettermark)

**Concept:** Geometric tech-focused lettermark.

- **Design:** A thick, rounded stroke forming a "C" shape, with a horizontal
  white rectangular element "completing" the data flow.
- **Rationale:** Focuses on brand recognition. The "terminal" or "cursor" style
  white block gives it a developer-centric, high-performance tech feel
  (reminiscent of Linear/Vercel styles).
- **Files:** `svg-02-lettermark-icon.svg`, `svg-02-lettermark-wordmark.svg`

## 03. Orbital / Atomic

**Concept:** Coordination through paths and orbits.

- **Design:** Concentric circles with a dashed outer orbit and a solid inner
  core. Small satellite nodes reside on the orbits.
- **Rationale:** Represents the "AI Ops" aspect—managing moving parts and
  autonomous agents orbiting a central project objective. It suggests precision
  and systemic organization.
- **Files:** `svg-03-orbital-icon.svg`, `svg-03-orbital-wordmark.svg`

## 04. Grid / Matrix

**Concept:** Structured organization and modularity.

- **Design:** A 2x2 grid of rounded squares with varying opacities. The
  bottom-right square is highlighted in white.
- **Rationale:** Directly references "organization" and "matrix" structures. The
  highlighted white block represents the current "focus" or the "AI-agent"
  active in the grid. It is highly scalable and works perfectly as a favicon.
- **Files:** `svg-04-grid-icon.svg`, `svg-04-grid-wordmark.svg`

---

_Note: All wordmarks use the Inter font (system fallback) with semi-bold
weighting and tight letter-spacing to match the modern "AI-native" aesthetic._
