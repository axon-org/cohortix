## Product Identity

### Name
**Cohortix** — Your AI allies, working together.

The name combines "Cohort" (a group of people working toward a common goal) with a modern suffix that suggests technology and coordination. It's approachable, memorable, and reflects our core value: teamwork between humans and AI.

### Tagline Options

1. **"Your AI crew, ready for action."**
   - Warm, energetic, action-oriented
   
2. **"Build your team. Launch your missions."**
   - Empowering, clear value proposition
   
3. **"The HQ for your AI allies."**
   - Playful, establishes the workspace metaphor

**Recommended:** Option 1 for primary marketing, Option 3 for product contexts.

## Terminology Table

We use friendly, mission-based language aligned with **PPV Pro productivity principles**. This makes AI collaboration feel like teamwork, not IT administration.

**Core Principle:** Humans and AI allies use the SAME terminology. There is no separate vocabulary.

**Hierarchy:** Domain → Vision → Mission → Operation / Rhythm → Task

### Core PPV Hierarchy (Alignment Zone)

From abstract → concrete:

| PPV Pro Original | Cohortix Term | Definition | Human Example | Ally Example |
|------------------|---------------|------------|---------------|--------------|
| Pillars & Purpose | **Domain** | Core life/expertise area that defines identity | "Family", "Health", "Business" | "Content Strategy", "Analytics" |
| Life Aspirations | **Vision** | Big emotional north star — the WHY | "Achieve financial freedom" | "Become the best content strategist" |
| Goals | **Mission** | Measurable outcome that serves a Vision | "Launch Cohortix and reach $50k MRR" | "Master Next.js 15 patterns" |
| Projects | **Operation** | Bounded initiative with start/end | "Build auth system" (Feb 1-15) | "Research 50 competitor channels" |
| Routines | **Rhythm** | Recurring habit with no end date | "Weekly business review" | "Daily learning cycle" |
| Actions | **Task** | Atomic unit of work | "Draft email copy" | "Summarize article on hooks" |

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Our Personality
- **Friendly** — Like a helpful teammate, not a corporate bot
- **Encouraging** — Celebrate wins, big and small
- **Clear** — No jargon, no buzzwords, just plain language
- **Playful** — Light touches of fun without being silly

### We Sound Like
- A supportive team lead
- A friend who's good at organizing things
- Notion, Canva, Slack — approachable product brands

### We Don't Sound Like
- An enterprise software manual
- A military command center (despite the mission theme)
- A generic AI company ("leverage our AI solutions")

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Onboarding** | Warm, welcoming | "Welcome to your new HQ! Let's recruit your first ally." |
| **Success states** | Celebratory | "Mission accomplished! 🎉 Your ally captured some great intel." |
| **Errors** | Helpful, not blaming | "Hmm, that mission hit a snag. Here's what we can try..." |
| **Empty states** | Encouraging | "Your cohort is ready and waiting. Recruit your first ally to get started." |
| **Loading** | Light, human | "Your allies are on it..." / "Briefing your cohort..." |

## Example Copy Snippets

### Onboarding Flow

**Welcome Screen:**
> Welcome to Cohortix! 👋
> 
> This is your HQ — the place where you and your AI allies work together to get things done.
> 
> Let's start by recruiting your first ally.

**First Ally Creation:**
> Give your ally a name and a specialty. Don't worry, you can always update these later.

**First Mission:**
> Nice! Your ally is standing by and ready for action.
> 
> Now, let's brief them on their first Mission. What measurable outcome would you like to achieve?

### Mission Control (Dashboard)

**Cohort Overview:**
> Your Cohort
> 3 allies standing by · 2 on mission · 0 off duty

**Mission Status:**
> Active Missions
> 5 missions in progress across 2 goals

**Intelligence Section:**
> Recent Intelligence
> Your cohort has captured 12 Insights this week. Search or browse below.

### Success Messages

**Mission Complete:**
> ✅ Mission accomplished!
> 
> Your ally finished the research goal and added 3 new insights to your intel.

**Ally Recruited:**
> 🎉 Welcome to the cohort!
> 
> Riley is ready for deployment. Brief them on their first Mission to get started.

### Error Messages

**Operation Failed:**
> ⚠️ This Operation hit a roadblock.
> 
> Don't worry — your ally saved their progress. Here's what happened:
> [Error details]
> 
> **What you can do:**
> - Retry the Operation
> - Break it into smaller Tasks
> - Brief a different ally

**Connection Issue:**
> 📡 Having trouble reaching your cohort.
> 
> This usually clears up on its own. If not, try refreshing the page.

### Empty States

**No Allies Yet:**
> Your cohort is empty!
> 
> Recruit your first ally to start tackling missions together.
> 
> [Recruit an Ally]

**No Missions:**
> All clear! No active Missions right now.
> 
> Ready to start something new?
> 
> [Start a Goal]

**No Intelligence:**
> Your Intelligence is empty for now.
> 
> As your allies complete Operations and Tasks, they'll capture Insights here. It's like a team knowledge base that builds itself.

## Writing Checklist

Before publishing any copy, check:

- [ ] Did I use Cohortix terminology? (Ally, Cohort, Mission, etc.)
- [ ] Would a non-technical person understand this?
- [ ] Is it warm and friendly, not robotic?
- [ ] Am I avoiding enterprise jargon? (leverage, utilize, synergy, optimize)
- [ ] Does it sound like something a friend would say?
- [ ] For errors: Am I being helpful, not blaming?
- [ ] For success: Am I celebrating the user's win?

## Words to Avoid

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|----------------|
| Execute | Deploy, Run |
| Leverage | Use |
| Utilize | Use |
| Optimize | Improve |
| Synergy | Teamwork |
| Workflow automation | Automated goals |
| Agent orchestration | Cohort coordination |
| Multi-tenant | Teams / Organizations / HQs |
| SaaS platform | Cohortix |
| End user | You |
| Functionality | Features |
| Robust | Strong, reliable |
| Seamless | Smooth, easy |
| Best-in-class | (just describe what it does) |

## Target Audience Reminder

**Who we're building for:**
- Everyday people who want AI help without the complexity
- Small teams and creators, not just enterprise IT
- People who like Notion, Canva, Figma — modern, friendly tools
- Non-technical users who want powerful results

**What they care about:**
- Getting things done, not managing infrastructure
- Clear results, not AI buzzwords
- Feeling in control, not overwhelmed
- Building something that grows with them
